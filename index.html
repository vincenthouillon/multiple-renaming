Multiple Renaming

A free, open-source, file renaming utility for macOS and Windows.
Download for macOS Download for Windows

Latest release v1.2

Between the photos recovered from your digital camera, your music, your videos or any other document that you may have to manage, your hard drives contain tens of thousands of files. To find your way, you can rename your files with an explicit name. Your music, your videos will have a uniform name, for example. When you need to rename one or two files, you can do it manually. However, it gets complicated when you have to rename dozens of files.

Use "Multiple Renaming" to change the names of many files at once. You can replace the file name, add the date, automatic file numbering, etc. You will have an immediate overview before applying your changes.
